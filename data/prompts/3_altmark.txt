% Definition: The Measure compensates the Undertaking(s) for fulfilling a specific Obligation.
compensation_for_obligation(Measure, Undertaking, Obligation).

% Definition: The Obligation is classified as a public service obligation.
public_service_obligation(Obligation).

% Definition: The obligation must be clearly defined. This means the nature, scope, and conditions under which it is to be performed are specified in sufficient detail.
clearly_defined(Obligation).

% Definition: The parameters for calculating compensation are set in advance, before the service is provided.
compensation_params_predefined(Measure, Obligation).

% Definition: The parameters for calculating compensation are based on objective criteria (e.g. cost formulas, output metrics).
compensation_params_objective(Measure, Obligation).

% Definition: The parameters for calculating compensation are publicly accessible and clearly communicated.
compensation_params_transparent(Measure, Obligation).

% Definition: The actual compensation amount granted under the Measure.
compensation_amount(Measure, Amount).

% Definition: The actual costs incurred in discharging the obligation.
actual_costs(Measure, Costs).

% Definition: The hypothetical cost of a well-run undertaking providing the same obligation.
hypothetical_cost(Measure, HypotheticalCosts).

% Definition: The reasonable profit allowed under the Measure.
reasonable_profit(Measure, Profit).

% Definition: A public procurement procedure was used to select the undertaking.
public_procurement_procedure_used(Measure, ProcurementProcedure).

% Definition: The public procurement procedure was designed to minimize cost to the community.
designed_to_minimise_cost_to_community(ProcurementProcedure).

% Exclusive: This predicate can only be parsed when compensation_amount(Measure, Amount), actual_costs(Measure, Costs), and reasonable_profit(Measure, Profit) were not parsed.  
% Definition: The text provides evidence that the compensation does not exceed the necessary costs plus a reasonable profit.
textual_evidence_compensation_does_not_exceed_necessary_costs_plus_reasonable_profit(Measure).

% Exclusive: This predicate can only be parsed when hypothetical_cost(Measure, HypotheticalCosts) was not parsed.
% Definition: The text provides evidence that the compensation is based on the cost structure of a well-run undertaking plus a reasonable profit.
textual_evidence_that_compensation_is_based_on_cost_structure_of_wellrun_plus_reasonable_profit(Measure).
