The Polish Ministry of Economy awarded on 4 April 2012 a direct grant to PCC MCAA Sp. Zo.o (hereinafter “PCC”) in the amount of PLN 66 995 312 (EUR 16 124 798.193), for an initial investment into a new plant producing ultra-pure monochloroacetic acid in Brzeg Dolny, in Lower Silesia. The eligible costs of this investment project were around PLN 223 317 710 (EUR 53 749 3285). The grant was financed through the general budget of the state.
