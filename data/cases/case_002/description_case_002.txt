On 19 February 2013 the Polish Ministry of Economy released permit NO 225. This permit entitled PCC MCAA Sp. Zo.o (hereinafter “PCC”) to conduct business activities in the “INVEST-PARK” Wałbrzych Special Economic Zone and invoked an exemptions from corporate income tax for income from the investment. The permit specified the maximum amount of eligible investment costs as PLN 300 000 000 (EUR 72 297 86710). The maximum amount of the tax exemption was not stated in the permit. The Polish authorities later published a calculation, stating that the maximum value of the corporate income tax exemption amounts to PLN 28 802 370 (EUR 6 941 166).
